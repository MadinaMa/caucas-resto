ABOUT US

Our restaurant is the first restaurant of Caucasian cuisine in the Canada. We offer a wide selection of authentic dishes inspired by our highly experienced chef. The cuisine is based on the culinary traditions of Caucasus developed over centuries. Our goal is to introduce Canadians to the traditions of this incredible place and give them the opportunity to experience the unique atmosphere of Caucasian mountains.
